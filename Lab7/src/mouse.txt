mouse 
A mouse (plural: mice) is a small mammal belonging to the order
of rodents. The best known mouse species is the common house mouse
(Mus musculus). It is also a popular pet. In some places, certain
kinds of field mice are also common. This rodent is eaten by most 
large carnivorous birds such as hawks and eagles. They are known 
to invade homes for food and occasionally shelter.
